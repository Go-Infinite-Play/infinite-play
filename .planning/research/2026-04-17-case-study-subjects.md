## 1. Talent Systems

**Correct name:** Talent Systems (talentsystems.com)

**Description:** Talent Systems is a global casting software company that describes itself as "powering the global casting software ecosystem." It operates a portfolio of casting and talent-management platforms used across film, television, commercials, theater, and digital production. It serves actors, casting directors, talent reps, studios, and independent filmmakers.

**Main products (all owned brands under the Talent Systems umbrella):**
- **Casting Networks** (flagship casting platform, North America)
- **Casting Frontier** (casting platform)
- **Spotlight** (UK's casting directory — not "SpotlightPro")
- **Tagmin** (agency management)
- **Staff Me Up** (unscripted / reality crewing)
- **Cast It Talent** and **Cast It Systems** (studio-grade casting tools)

**Scale:** 2M+ talent profiles, 15M+ auditions scheduled, 30K+ casting professionals on-platform.

**Positioning / visual identity:** Enterprise-B2B SaaS tone; each sub-brand retains its own logo but the parent wordmark is clean sans-serif. Industry positioning is "all the major casting platforms under one roof."

## 4. Hidden Client

**Correct name:** Hidden Client (hiddenclient.com). Tagline: "Turn Job Postings Into Your Best Leads."

**Description:** Hidden Client is an AI-powered lead-generation service for services firms. It monitors job postings across LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Glassdoor, and company career pages; every morning at 7am it delivers a short email with matched leads — company, decision-maker contact, the triggering job posting, and a personalized outreach draft written in the user's voice.

**Thesis:** A company posting a full-time role for the work you already do is a high-intent lead. Surface those postings the day they go live.

**Target customers:** Fractional and outsourced services firms — fractional CFO/CMO/compliance practices, outsourced sales/HR/legal/IT agencies, construction PM consultancies, and similar.

**Key features:**
- Daily 7am inbox delivery (no dashboard, no login)
- Full match context (company, title, posting date)
- Decision-maker contact (LinkedIn + business email)
- Personalized outreach draft per lead, in user's voice
- Monthly tuning based on fit feedback

**Pricing:** $497/month with a 60-day money-back guarantee; founders pricing locks in $100 off for life ($397/mo).

**Positioning:** Differentiates against enterprise intent platforms (ZoomInfo, 6sense), DIY stacks (Clay, Apollo), and outsourced recruiters — the wedge is job-postings-as-signal plus done-for-you delivery for small services firms.
